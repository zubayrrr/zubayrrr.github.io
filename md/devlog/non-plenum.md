---
   
   
- Also known as PVC.   
- As opposed to [Plenum](../devlog/plenum.md) cabeling, Non-Plenum is a normal [UTP](../devlog/utp.md)/[STP](../devlog/stp.md) rated cable.   
- It cannot be used in raised floors, ceilings or walls.
