- [Fiber Optic](../devlog/fiber%20optic.md)   
  - Higher bandwidth   
  - Longer distances   
  - Immune to EMI   
  - Better security   
  - 40 Km+   
  - 69 Tbps   
- Copper/[Coax](../devlog/coax.md), [Twisted Pair](../devlog/twisted%20pair.md)   
  - Less expensive   
  - Easy to install   
  - Inexpensive tools   
  - \< 100 m   
  - 10 Gbps
